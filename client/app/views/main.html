Introduction

This website is called CrowdLEC. It is used to crowdsourse data about people's living expenses in China. CrowdLEC does statistics on people's living expenses in months. You are able to check detailed living expenses by choose links of different areas. There are also a ranking of total expenses and some data visualization. Two different type of incentives are designed on this website. The first one is send you a voucher code to your mailing box after you post your living expenses data(actually use a random number to substitute). The second one is you are able to share this website to social network(facebook).
